Center of Circle

How to construct a Circle's Center
using just a compass and a straightedge

Steps:

  • Draw a line across the circle to make a "chord"
  • Construct the perpendicular bisector of that chord to make a diameter of the circle
  • Construct the perpendicular bisector of that diameter to get the center of the circle
