Practice solving triangles.

What Does "AAS", "ASA" etc Mean?

It means which sides or angles we already know:


AAA
Three Angles

AAS
Two Angles and a Side not between

ASA
Two Angles and a Side between

SAS
Two Sides and an Angle between

SSA
Two Sides and an Angle not between

SSS
Three Sides
