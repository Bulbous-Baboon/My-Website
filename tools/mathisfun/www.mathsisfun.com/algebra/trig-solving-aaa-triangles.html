Solving AAA Triangles

"AAA" means "Angle, Angle, Angle"

"AAA" is when we know all three angles of a triangle, but no sides.

AAA triangles are impossible to solve further since there is nothing to show us size ... we know the shape but not how big it is.

We need to know at least one side to get any further ... that's life!
