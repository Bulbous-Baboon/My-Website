Data Entry

Enter your matrix in the cells below "A" or "B".

Or you can type in the big output area and press "to A" or "to B" (the calculator will try its best to interpret your data).

Example: Enter

1, 2, 3
3, 1, 4
,, 5

And press "to A"

SAVING

To save your matrix press "from A" or "from B" and then copy and paste the resulting text somewhere safe. When you come back just paste it and press "to A" or "to B".
