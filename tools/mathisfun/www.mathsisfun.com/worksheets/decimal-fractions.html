Decimal Fraction Worksheets

Addition :: Subtraction :: Multiplication :: Division :: Conversion

The following worksheets all use "Decimal Fractions", in other words tenths, hundredths, etc. This makes them a little easier to work with.

But remember to simplify your answer (example: 50100 becomes 12).
