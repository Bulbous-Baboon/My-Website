2D Shapes

Regular Polygons

A polygon is a plane (2D) shape with straight sides.
To be a regular polygon all the sides and angles must be the same:


Triangle - 3 Sides
Square - 4 Sides

Pentagon - 5 Sides
Hexagon - 6 sides

Heptagon - 7 Sides
Octagon - 8 Sides

Nonagon - 9 Sides
Decagon - 10 Sides
More ...

 

Other Common Polygons

These are 2D shapes, and they are polygons, but not regular polygons:


Quadrilateral
Any 4 sided 2D shape
Rectangle - 4 Sides
All right angles
And many more!

 

Curved Shapes

These are also 2D shapes, but are not polygons because they have curves:


Circle
Ellipse
And many more!
