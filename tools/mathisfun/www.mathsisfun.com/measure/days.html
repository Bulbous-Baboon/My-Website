AM/PM vs 24 Hour Clock

There are two ways to show the time: "AM/PM" or "24 Hour Clock".

A Typical Day

This is how my friend spends his day:

  • He wakes up early in the morning, gets dressed, and has breakfast
  • then he goes to school
  • in the middle of the day he has lunch
  • in the afternoon he comes home from school
  • then he does homework and has some time to play
  • then just as the sun goes down he has his evening meal
  • then he might watch TV or play on the computer
  • and then he goes to sleep

Days Have Names

There are seven different days:

  • Monday
  • Tuesday
  • Wednesday
  • Thursday
  • Friday
  • Saturday
  • Sunday

7 days together make a Week.
