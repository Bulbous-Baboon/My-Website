Activity: Day of the Week Born

 

Conduct a Survey to find if there is any truth behind this Rhyme:

Monday's child is fair of face,
Tuesday's child is full of grace,
Wednesday's child is full of woe,
Thursday's child has far to go.
Friday's child is loving and giving,
Saturday's child works hard for a living,
But the child born on the Sabbath Day,
Is fair and wise and good in every way.

Use the Day of the Week application to find which day of the week each person was born.

 

Go through each step of Conducting a Survey:

  • Step one: create the questions
  • Step two: ask the questions
  • Step three: tally the results
  • Step four: present the results

It should be very interesting asking the questions ... have fun!
