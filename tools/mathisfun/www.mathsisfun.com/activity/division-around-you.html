Division Examples In the World Around You

Look for examples of division in the world around you.

Example: A Box of 12 Eggs                         

There are 12 eggs in 2 rows of 6:

  • so 12 divided by 2 is 6
  • and 12 divided by 6 is 2

Sketch the division you find, and show both division facts.

Look for things like ice cubes in a tray, chocolates in a box, tiles on a wall, paving stones, etc.
