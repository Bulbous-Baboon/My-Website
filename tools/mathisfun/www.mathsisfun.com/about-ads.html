Hiding Ads

Ads can sometimes be distracting, so you can hide them for 36 hours (yes they come back, but you can hide them again).

Handy for classroom demonstrations!

Click on the "Hide Ads" near the top right.

 

Seen An Ad You Don't Like?

We do our best to ensure only good ads are shown, but sometimes a bad ad slips through.

So please let us know of any ad you don't think should be there. Give as many details as possible (name and web address of advertiser, what page it appeared on, and a screenshot would be good) so it can be identified and blocked.
