Definition of

Dimension

more ...
A measurement of length in one direction.

Examples: width, depth and height are dimensions.

A line has one dimension (1D),
a square has two dimensions (2D),
and a cube has three dimensions (3D).



In Physics it can also mean any physical measurement such as length, time, mass, etc.
