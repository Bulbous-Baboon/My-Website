Definition of

Order Of Magnitude

more ...
Size of a value by approximate factors of 10.

Examples:
• the order of magnitude of 12 is 1 because it is around 10 in size.
• the order of magnitude of 170 is 2 because it is around 100 in size.
• a kilometer (1000 meters) is three orders of magnitude greater than a meter.


Can also be used to mean "very much" bigger or smaller.
Example: "Sam's tennis game is an order of magnitude better this year"
