Definition of

Quadrillion

more ...

A thousand million millions.

1,000 x 1,000,000 x 1,000,000 = 1,000,000,000,000,000

Which is a 1 followed by 15 zeros

Using scientific notation: 1 × 1015

We could also think of it as a thousand trillion or a million billion.

Examples:
• there are about a quadrillion ants on Earth (maybe 10 times more or less, no one knows exactly)

The prefix "peta-" means a quadrillion. Example: a petameter is a quadrillion meters.


(In many non-English speaking countries it means a million million million million, which is a 1 followed by 24 zeros, or 1 × 1024)
