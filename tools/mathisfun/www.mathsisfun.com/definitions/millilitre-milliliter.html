Definition of

Millilitre | Milliliter

more ...
A Metric unit of volume

Equal to 1/1,000 (one-thousandth) of a liter

Used for small amounts of liquid

The abbreviation is ml or mL

It takes about 20 drops (from a standard eye dropper) to make one mL

This bottle can measure 2,000 ml (2 liters) in 250 ml increments.
