Definition of

Latitude

Any of the imaginary lines that run around the Earth.

It shows how far North or South from the Equator.

The Equator is at 0° latitude, and the latitudes increase up to 90° at the poles.

Together with the longitude we can locate a precise position on the Earth.

(To help you remember that it runs side to side think "flat-itude".)
