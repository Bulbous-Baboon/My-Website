Alphabet Numbers Puzzle - Solution

The Puzzle:

Using any letter only once, what are the largest and smallest numbers that you can write down in words?

Example: EIGHTY
But not NINETY as N is used twice


Bonus 1: allow negatives such as MINUS TWO

Bonus 2: allow calculations such as TWO SQUARED

Our Solution:

Largest: FIVE THOUSAND
Smallest: ZERO or NOUGHT


And for our Indian friends:
From Chiranjeev: the largest number is TEN LAC, and smallest is MINUS FORTY LAC.
From Krishna Sai Vootla: the largest number is FORTY LAKHS
From Rahul Jha: the largest number is EIGHTY FOUR LAC

Bonus 1: allow negatives
Smallest: MINUS FORTY

Bonus 2: allow calculations
Largest: SIXTY CUBED (=216,000) from Steven Nguyen
