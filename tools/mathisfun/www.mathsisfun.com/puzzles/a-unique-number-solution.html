A Unique Number Puzzle - Solution

The Puzzle:

What is unique about 8549176320 ?

Our Solution:

It is the digits 0 to 9 in alphabetical order.

Note: it can also be exactly divided by all of the digits 1-9 except 7 (thanks to "The Hermit")
