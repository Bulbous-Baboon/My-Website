Adding Fractions Illustrated

There are 3 Simple Steps to add fractions:

  • Step 1: Make sure the bottom numbers are the same (so the slices are the same size)
  • Step 2: Add the top numbers. Put the answer over the same bottom number.
  • Step 3: Simplify the fraction (if needed).
