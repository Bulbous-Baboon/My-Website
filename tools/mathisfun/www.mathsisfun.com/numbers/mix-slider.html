Mix Slider

Let's say you want a mixture to come out a certain way ...

  • Mix a 10% solution and a 30% solution to get a 15% solution
  • Drive at 30 km/h, then drive at 50 km/h for an average of 45 km/h

And you want to know how much of each you need? This will help!

 

Example: Mix a 10% and 30% solution to get a 15% solution

Type 10 for the left hand side, 30 for the right hand side, adjust the slider to 15, and then put a Quantity of 100 (if you want 100 ml):

  • 75 ml of 10% solution
  • 25 ml of 30% solution

Will get you 100 ml of 15% solution
