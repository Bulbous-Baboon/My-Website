Prime Numbers Activity

Make your own prime number chart!

Click on any number:

  • its multiples get marked blue (definitely composite numbers).
  • it is marked gold if it might be a prime number,

Keep marking numbers, until finally you will have a list of only prime numbers and composite numbers

Oh, and 1 is neither prime nor composite, so you can't mark it.
