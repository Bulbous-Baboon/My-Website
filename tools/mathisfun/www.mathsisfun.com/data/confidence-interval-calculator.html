Standard Deviation and Mean

Use the Standard Deviation Calculator to calculate your sample's standard deviation and mean.

Caution: you should really use the standard deviation of the entire population!

But you can use the standard deviation of your sample if you have enough observations: at least n=30, the more the better.

How It Is Calculated

The Confidence Interval formula is

x̄ ± Z s√n

Where:

  • x̄ is the mean
  • Z is the Z-value from the table below
  • s is the standard deviation
  • n is the number of observations
  Z
80% 1.282
85% 1.440
90% 1.645
95% 1.960
99% 2.576
99.5% 2.807
99.9% 3.291
